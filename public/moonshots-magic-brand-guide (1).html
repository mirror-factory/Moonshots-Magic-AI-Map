06. Usage Rules

Do

  • Always use the blue ampersand (&) as the visual anchor
  • Maintain generous clear space around the logo
  • Use dark backgrounds as the default context
  • Use the black monochrome variant on light backgrounds
  • Keep the wordmark in uppercase -- it is part of the identity

Don't

  • Never change the ampersand color independently
  • Never rotate, stretch, or distort the wordmark
  • Never place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Never use gradients or effects on the logo
  • Never mix logo variants in the same composition
