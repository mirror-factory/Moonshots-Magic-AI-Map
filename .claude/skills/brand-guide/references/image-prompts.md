# Image Generation Prompts

All prompts must end with the mandatory suffix for consistent black-and-white grainy aesthetic.

## Mandatory Suffix

Append to ALL image prompts:

```
, monochromatic black and white, NO COLOR, heavy visible film grain texture throughout entire image, high ISO noise, scratched aged film stock, deep pure blacks (#000000), bright stark whites, extreme contrast ratio, 1970s-1980s analog photography aesthetic, vintage NASA archival quality, visible dust particles and scratches, halftone dot pattern subtly visible, matte finish not glossy, desaturated completely, photographic paper texture
```

## Subject Prompts

### Space Shuttle

```
NASA space shuttle orbiter photographed from dramatic low angle showing underbelly and wing detail, craft positioned against pure black void of space, harsh directional lighting creating strong shadows, thermal tiles visible with texture detail, rocket engines prominent, composition reminiscent of Stanley Kubrick 2001 A Space Odyssey cinematography, archival NASA photograph circa 1981-1985, technical documentation style
```

### Moon

```
Detailed full moon photograph showing crater texture and maria surface features, captured through telescope, sharp focus on lunar surface detail, terminator line partially visible showing depth of craters, pure black space background with no stars, style of Apollo mission archival lunar photography, scientific documentation aesthetic, large format film quality
```

### Astronaut

```
Apollo-era astronaut in full EVA spacesuit, helmet visor reflecting light, figure photographed in dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, suit texture and life support equipment visible in detail, heroic portrait composition, background pure black void, NASA official portrait style circa 1969-1972, Hasselblad medium format camera quality, slight motion blur on edges
```

### Stars & Deep Space

```
Deep space starfield photograph, thousands of stars of varying brightness and size, some stars showing slight diffraction spikes from telescope optics, pure black void background, subtle nebula wisps barely visible, long exposure astrophotography style, observatory telescope capture, Palomar Observatory archival aesthetic, scientific star chart quality
```

### Planet / Celestial Body

```
Planet surface or celestial body photographed from orbit or flyby mission, showing terrain features, craters, or atmospheric bands in stark detail, harsh sunlight creating dramatic terminator shadow line, Voyager or Pioneer mission archival photography style, JPL official release aesthetic, technical scientific documentation
```

### Rocket / Launch

```
Saturn V or similar heavy lift rocket on launch pad or during ascent, exhaust plume creating dramatic smoke and fire contrast, launch tower structure visible, sense of massive scale, Kennedy Space Center documentation photography style, press release archival quality, dramatic upward angle composition
```
